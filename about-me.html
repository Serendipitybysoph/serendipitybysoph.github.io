About Me

Hello!

I'm Sophia, the girl behind the camera. I was that girl in high school who brought her point-and-shoot everywhere and came home with 500 pictures. I've since upgraded my gear but my love for photography has not changed.

For me, it is all about capturing that one snapshot in time - that serendipitous moment in which the pieces of the puzzle just seem to fall into place. My goal is for your photos to instantly transport you back to that micro-moment in time to relive the joyous feelings and memories, and to tell a story about where you've been.

I live in the northern Virginia area with my springer spaniel and you can find me out running, hiking, being a foodie, or curled up with a good book.
